About the Drug Inventory System

Our mission is to simplify pharmacy operations with a clean, fast, and reliable stock management experience—helping teams avoid stockouts, track expiries, and streamline supplier relations.

Why we built this

Pharmacies handle thousands of SKUs with varying expiries and storage conditions. We designed a system that prioritizes clarity, speed, and accuracy.

  • Real-time search and filtering
  • Clear visibility of expiries and low stock
  • Supplier tracking to prevent delays
  • Responsive UI for mobile devices
